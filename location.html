Camp Again is Heading to Vermont!

Our Labor Day 2024 Hosts: Camp Kiniya, Colchester VT

Founded over 100 years ago by Helen Van Buren, Kiniya is a camp situated on the picturesque shores of Lake Champlain in Colchester, Vermont.

Camp Kiniya's location in Colchester ensures that our campers are surrounded by lush greenery, wooded areas, and the serene ambiance of the lake. This 146 acre campus allows Camp Again to enjoy beautiful waterfront along the shores of Lake Champlain, one of the largest freshwater lakes in North America, with beautiful views of Vermont’s Green Mountains.

Here's a Few Reasons Why Camp Again Chose to Partner with Kiniya:

  1. Modern and updated dining hall, camper cabins, and performance spaces.
  2. Access to miles of stunning waterfront for paddle boarding, kayaking, canoeing, waterskiing, and more!
  3. Soccer, Basketball, and Lacrosse Fields. Tennis and Pickleball Courts. Archery
  4. Low and High Ropes Courses

Burlington Vermont

Looking to hangout in Vermont Before or After Camp?

Only 20 minutes away from our 2024 location, the city of Burlington Vermont is a perfect place for extending your stay before of after camp. Known for it's picturesque views, walk-able downtown, and thriving arts and culture scene, Burlington’s charm is undeniable.

Visitors to Burlington will never be bored! Explore the Church Street Marketplace to do some shopping while listening to live music. Visit the ECHO Leahy Center to learn about the region's ecology and history. enjoy the delicious local food this city has to offer! Or even walk or bike along the lakefront! Burlington also hosts a large number of craft beer and cider breweries, making it a great place to get a drink. Burlington offers a mix of modern amenities and New England charm, making it a perfect place to extend your adventure.

Camp Again would love to provide more information and recommendations for lodging and restaurants upon request. Contact us for more details!
