Packing List

Here's a guide to help you pack for the weekend!

Essentials:

  • Photo ID, Copy of Confirmation Email and Details Sheet
  • Credit Card/Cash for Extra Spending
  • Phone and Charger (To take Photos of Course!!)

Clothing and Accessories:

  • T Shirts*/ Tanks
  • Shorts
  • Long Sleeve Shirt/ Pants
  • Bathing Suit
  • Flip Flops/ Crocs/ Chacos/ Sandals
  • Athletic Sneakers
  • Rain Jacket/ Poncho
  • Hat or Sunglasses
  • Fun Outfits/ Costumes (optional)
  • *Cabin Color Wars T-Shirt Will Be Provided Upon Arrival

Personal Items:

  • Personal Toiletries (Toothbrush, Toothpaste, Shampoo, Conditioner, Bodywash, Menstrual Products, Etc.)
  • Sunscreen
  • Bug Spray
  • Towels (Bathing, Beach)
  • Personal Medications
  • Perscription Glasses/ Contacts
  • Backpack (optional)

Cabin Essentials

  • Sleeping Bag or Twin Bedding for bunk beds
  • Pillow
  • Blanket
  • Flashlight or Headlamp
  • Reusable Water Bottle

Tech/Entertainment:

  • Phone Charges
  • Portable Speaker (optional)
  • Book or Kindle (optional)
  • Playing Cards (optional)
  • Musical Instruments (optional)
