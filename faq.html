  • As we are serving alcoholic beverages all campers must be 21+. Please bring identification when you go to camp as it will be checked upon arrival at check-in.

  • Camp Again offers a variety of activities over the weekend. While we do like to keep most of what we'll get up to a secret, here is a general list of what you might expect:
    Sports: Such as Archery, Tennis, Soccer, Pickleball, Kickball, and Basketball
    Thrill Activities: Rock Climbing, High and Low Ropes Courses, and the Giant Swing
    Waterfront: Explore the serene lake with kayacking, paddleboarding, canoeing, swimming, waterskiing
    Camp Again Classics: Giant Hungry Hungry Hippos, Capture the Flag, Gold Rush, and other all camp games and extravaganzas
    Nightime Activities: Epic themed parties under the stars that features our included Camp Again Bar!

  • While in session, campers will sleep in cabins on the property.
    - These modern cabins have twin bunk beds. You will either need to bring a sleeping bag or linens as they will not be provided.
    - Some cabins will have bathrooms connected to them while others will not. Cabins without connected bathrooms will have a facility nearby to use.

  • It depends.

  • By Car:
    - If you prefer a road trip, driving to Vermont offers flexibility and the opportunity to enjoy scenic routes. The state is well-connected by highways as well as ferries.
    - Camp Again 2024 will be located at the follwoing address: 1317 Camp Kiniya Road, Colchester VT, 05446

    By Train:
    - Amtrak's Vermonter line connects Washington, D.C., and St. Albans, Vermont, with stops in Burlington. There are also trains that leave from Penn Station in NYC.
    - The downtown Burlington Train Station provides a charming arrival point into Vermont. Camp Again on opening day will be doing scheduled pickups at both the train station as well as the airport.

    By Plane:
    - Burlington International Airport (BTV) is the primary airport serving the area surrounding camp. Located only 30 minutes away from camp, this is the closest airport besides flying into Montreal.
    - Many airlines offer direct flights to Burlington from major cities, providing convenient access for participants flying in.

    Transportation from Burlington VT to Camp Again
    - For campers arriving by air or train, counselors will provide pickups at designated times. This flexibility ensures that regardless of your mode of transportation, the journey to camp is convenient and stress free.
    - More details will come about those pickup times after tickets are bought.

  • Camp Again will provide three meals a day plus beverage, sncks and deserts to our campers. These meals will be cooked daily by our Kitchen Crew and will be served in the dining hall
    - Meals will feature a mix of childhood camp classics and gourmet delights.
    - All Dietary Needs will be accomodated, just let us know when you're signing up!

  • Camp Again has a detailed packing list on our website.
    - The attire needed for camp is casual, athletic, and comfortable. Most people bring some kind of sneaker and casual shoe (croc, flip-flop, teva, chacos, etc.) to wear throughout the weekend.
    - Nicer more dressy clothes are only needed for our closing award ceremony, so just one of those outfits is good to pack.
    - Keep in mind that while the event is held on Labor Day Weekend when it typically is quite warm during the day, at night in Vermont it can get quite chilly, so it's a good idea to bring long pants, a light jacket, or sweats.

  • Camp Again has a refund policy. Specific details can be found during the registration process.
