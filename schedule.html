Sample Schedule

Here's what a typical day at Camp Again might look like:

Day 1: Friday - Arrival Day

  • 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Check-In at Camp Again, Get Swag Bags, Move into Cabins
  • 5:15 PM - 7:00 PM: Camp Orientation and Welome Dinner
  • 7:15 PM - 9:00 PM: Icebreaker Games and Team Bonding on the Main Field
  • 9:15 PM - 11:00 PM: Campfire Kickoff and Themed Party #1 on Junior Beach (Theme TBD)

Day 2: Saturday - Cabin vs Cabin Color Wars

  • 8:30 AM - 9:00 AM Morning Dip Offered in the Lake at Senior Beach
  • 9:00 AM - 9:30 AM: Breakfast in the Dining Hall
  • 9:45 AM - 12:00 PM: Morning Color Wars Competitions (Camp Classics)
  • 12:15 PM - 1:45 PM: Lunch and Rest Hour under the Pines
  • 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Afternoon color Wars Competition (Camp Again Specials)
  • 5:15 PM - 6:15 PM: Relax and Unwind back at Cabins
  • 6:30 PM - 7:15 PM: Dinner at the Dining Hall
  • 7:30 PM - 9:00 PM: Evening Activity/ Trip to Seb's Snack Bar for Ice Cream
  • 9:15 PM - End of Day Themed Party #2 (Theme TBD) in theater

Day 3: Sunday - Choice Activities and Lip Sync Battle

  • 8:30 AM - 9:00 AM Morning Dip Offered in the Lake at Senior Beach
  • 9:00 AM - 9:30 AM: Breakfast in the Dining Hall
  • 9:45 AM - 12:00 PM: Waterfront Fun, Beach Games
  • 12:15 PM - 1:45 PM: Lunch and Rest Hour under the Pines
  • 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Optional Green Mountain Day Hike, Activity Free Choice
  • 5:15 PM - 6:15 PM: Relax and Unwind back at Cabins
  • 6:30 PM - 7:15 PM: Dinner at the Dining Hall
  • 7:30 PM - 9:15 PM: All Camp Lip Sync Battle
  • 9:30 PM - End of Day: Themed Party #3 at Junior Beach

Day 4: Monday - Last Day and Departure

  • 8:30 AM - 9:00 AM Morning Dip Offered in the Lake at Senior Beach
  • 9:00 AM - 9:30 AM: Breakfast in the Dining Hall
  • 9:45 AM - 11:00 PM: Color Wars Finale
  • 11:15 AM - 12:00 PM: Cabin Clean Up
  • 12:15 PM - 1:45 PM: Lunch and Awards Ceremony
  • 2:15 PM - 4:00 PM: Check-Out and See Ya Laters
